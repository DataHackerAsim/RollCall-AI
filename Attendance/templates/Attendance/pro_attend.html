System Information

This system automatically marks attendance by recognizing students from a live stream or uploaded picture.

Key Features

  • Automatic face recognition marks students as Present (P)
  • Non-recognized students remain Absent (A)
  • Drag unidentified faces to student names for manual assignment
  • Click P/A indicators to manually toggle attendance
  • False positive correction available
  • Section-based filtering for large classes
  • Support for multiple batches and sections
  • Enrollment-based attendance tracking
